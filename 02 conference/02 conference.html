Seattle Typography Conference 2017

October 20th to the 22nd

Hosted by Talaris Conference Center

Font-fanatics and letter-lovers from across the country are gathering in Seattle for the Annual Seattle Typography Conference,October 20th to the 22nd! Join us for a jam-packed event to learn more about lettering, typography, and font design.

The Seattle Typography Conference is an annual conference presented by the non-profit Society of Typographic Aficionados (SOTA), an international organization dedicated to the promotion, study, and support of typography and related arts.
